Details Itinerary Things To Carry {# About#}
A landlocked state, majorly covered by the Vindhyachal range is Chattisgarh, a less explored wondrous tourist destination. Take part in a 10 day tribal tour of Chattisgarh which will take you to explore the tribes living in the deep forests of the state.
Bastar is the epitome of tribal area of Chattisgarh where you will be able to find numerous tribal communities. Arrive in Delhi to meet the fellow travellers and travel to Raipur on the second day. Get warmly welcomed to the land of waterfalls, deep forest ranges, hills, caves and silent rivers. This tour would be a research type to know the lifestyle of an unknown set of people living in the interiors of a major state of India.
Witness the coruscant palaces, meet the royal members, interact with the tribal people, watch their traditions, munch on the local cuisines, get amused at the preserved culture of the forest and fall in love with their peaceful life in harmony with nature

  1. Day 1 - Raipur-Jagdalpur
    Upon your arrival in Raipur, the representative of the tour provider will meet you and then get transferred to the hotel.
    Freshen up and leave for Jagdalpur.
    En route, enjoy a picnic lunch at one restaurant.
    Jagdalpur is a unique beauty which has numerous tribal villages and mind blowing wildlife of Chattisgarh.
    Check in to the hotel after arrival and relax.
  2. Day 2 - Jagdalpur - Kanger Valley National Park
    After having breakfast, leave to visit yet another attraction of the state, Kanger Valley National Park.
    It is known for its rare wildlife species and the pristine surroundings.
    This is considered to be one of the finest and virgin forest areas.
    Visit the large deposits of limestone which are present in the form of caves in the park which enhance the beauty of the park.
    Enjoy picnic lunch near the Tiratgarh Fall which is a famous tourist attraction.
    Return to your hotel afterwards.
  3. Day 3 - Chitrakoot Falls - Nagarnar- Jagdalpur
    Post breakfast, head out for a sightseeing tour of the famous attractions of the place.
    First and foremost, you will visit the Chitrakoot Falls, famous as the ‘Niagara Falls of India’
    After sightseeing, proceed to Jagdalpur back.
  4. Day 4 - Jagdalpur
    In the morning, post breakfast, leave for ‘haat’ which is the weekly market of Jagdalpur.
    Witness the mesmerising local tribal life which the haat showcases.
    Post lunch at the hotel, spend the rest of the day at leisure.
    You can explore the place or take rest at the hotel.
  5. Day 5 - Jagdalpur – Narayanpur – Raipur
    After having your breakfast,start your journey back to Raipur.
    En-route, take a side trip to Narayanpur.
    Narayanpur has a large collection of some unique handicrafts.
    Handicrafts from this place are known as the best in the Bastar region.
    It offers many items such as bell metal, wrought iron, woodcraft and bamboo artifacts.
    Also, make your way to the Ramakrishna Mission Center which is a major tourist attraction at Narayanpur.
    Upon arrival in Raipur, check-in to the hotel and have a comfortable stay at night.
  6. Day 6 - Raipur
    This is the last day of the tour.
    Have your breakfast and then spend the rest of the day at leisure.
    Later, take a transfer to the airport and board a flight for your onward journey.
